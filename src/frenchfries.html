Recipe Name: French Fries

Time: 60 min

Description: Best homemade fries in an hour!

Ingredients: potatoes, vegetable oil, salt

Instructions:
1. Slice the potatoes into sticks.
2. Soak potatoes in water for an hour.
3. Dry the potato sticks
4. Heat oil to 300 degrees F and fry potato sticks
5. Place the french fries on paper towels to soak excess oil.
6. Refry the french fries at 400 degrees F.
7. Remove french fries once done, lightly sprinkle with salt to taste and server
